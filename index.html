What We Have Done

Lin Dan is one of the greatest badminton players of all time, winning two Olympic gold medals in men's singles at Beijing 2008 and London 2012. He also secured five BWF World Championships titles (2006, 2007, 2009, 2011, 2013) and six All England Open titles. Lee Chong Wei, his longtime rival, earned three Olympic silver medals (2008, 2012, 2016) and three World Championship silvers. Kento Momota, a dominant force in the late 2010s, won two World Championships (2018, 2019) and numerous Superseries titles. Lee Zii Jia claimed the All England Open title in 2021, marking his breakthrough. Meanwhile, Viktor Axelsen, the reigning Olympic champion, won gold at Tokyo 2020 and secured the 2017 and 2022 World Championship titles, solidifying his place among badminton's elite.
